How it works

Four steps from blank page to a fully explained hiring decision. No login, no database — runs entirely in your session.

01

Name the role

Enter the job title you are hiring for. This anchors the entire analysis and appears in the written narrative, on all exports, and in the PDF report.

e.g. "Senior Software Engineer" · "Marketing Manager"
02

Define criteria and weights

Add every factor that matters. Assign a weight and mark whether lower values are better. The engine handles normalisation — you just set priorities.

⚖️
Weights — any whole number. Higher weight = more influence on the final score.
↓
Cost criteria — mark Salary or Notice Period as lower is better. Scoring is automatically inverted.
🎯
Smart scale detection — 255 keyword patterns covering 35+ types: Salary, CGPA, Experience, GRE, CIBIL, IQ, EQ, Notice Period and more.
📐
Custom range — override any scale with your own min/max if needed.
255 keyword patterns · 35+ scale types
03

Add candidates

Upload a CSV for bulk entries or add candidates one by one. Provide the raw value per criteria — the engine handles all scoring from there.

📤
CSV bulk upload — 100+ candidates at once. Columns map to your criteria. Mismatches flagged before scoring.
✏️
Manual entry — add candidates one at a time through a simple form.
🔢
Shortlist size — set how many top candidates to highlight. Full pool available in export.
100+ candidates · Review before scoring
04

Get your ranked, explained result

The scoring engine runs instantly. Every candidate gets a weighted score, a rank, and a full breakdown across six interconnected views.

🏅
Ranked list — all candidates ordered by weighted score with gap badges.
📋
Written narrative — who won, what drove the score, priority alignment, confidence level.
🏆
Best per criteria — who topped each individual factor, separate from overall rank.
🔀
What-if sliders — adjust any weight live and watch the ranking update instantly.
🟢
Stability check — sensitivity testing across ±10% weight shifts. Flags weight-sensitive decisions.
📄
Export — full ranked list as CSV or formatted PDF with score breakdowns and narrative.
Fully explainable · No black box · Export ready
